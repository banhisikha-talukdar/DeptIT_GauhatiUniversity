About

Gauhati University (GU) is one of the premier institutions of higher education in Northeast India, established in 1948. The university has been at the forefront of academic excellence and innovation for over seven decades.

Great Sanskrit scholar, Indologist and philanthropist Krishna Kanta Handiqui was the founder Vice Chancellor of the university. Under his visionary leadership, the university laid strong foundations for academic excellence.

Being the oldest and the largest university in the entire Northeast, the university represents the aspirations and dreams of millions in the region, serving as a beacon of knowledge and progress.

Gauhati University has been serving as the Think Tank for development of entire Assamese Society and the Northeast region, contributing significantly to education, research, and social development.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

The vision of the Department of Information Technology, Gauhati University is to establish a structured seat of learning for inclusive, adaptive, futuristic, and need-oriented studies and research in the domain-specific as well as interdisciplinary areas of Information Technology. To achieve the vision, the department is engaged in creating and nurturing an ecosystem for the systematic study of Information Technology, with embedded learning of science, humanities, and skills, thereby promoting a generation of worthy human and knowledge resources.

Our Mission

  • To impart different programs of studies at UG, PG, and research levels, with a contemporary and up-to-date curriculum, fulfilling the need of a vast pool of local aspiring students.
  • To facilitate hands-on training facilities through a formal schedule as well as an open and unrestricted environment for acquiring and enhancing knowledge and skills on competent technologies.
  • To adopt regular evaluations of the curriculum and the course content, and to take measures for required revisions for implantations.
  • To create opportunities for add-on knowledge and skills, for enhancing competencies of students, through regular activities of academic and industry interaction.
  • To encourage solution and industry-oriented semester-long projects, for creating a pool of employable human resources on completion of their programs.
  • To encourage output-oriented research on region-specific problems and requirements, as well as contemporary and state-of-the-art universal problems, including interdisciplinary areas.

Infrastructure Facilities

ICT Enabled Class Room

Modern classrooms equipped with projectors, smart boards, and audio-visual systems for interactive learning experiences.

Seminar Room

Well-furnished seminar hall with seating capacity for conferences, workshops, and academic presentations.

Server Room

Climate-controlled data center housing high-performance servers with redundant power supply and network infrastructure.

IoT and Embedded System Facility

Equipped with microcontrollers, sensors, development boards, and prototyping tools for IoT project development.

AI and NLP Research Laboratory

Dedicated research facility with GPU clusters and specialized software for artificial intelligence and natural language processing projects.

Animation and Graphics Laboratory

Professional workstations with industry-standard software for 3D modeling, animation, and multimedia content creation.

Cryptography Laboratory

Specialized facility for studying encryption algorithms, network security protocols, and cybersecurity applications.

Green Energy: 30 KW Solar Power System

Sustainable solar power installation providing clean energy and reducing the department's carbon footprint.
